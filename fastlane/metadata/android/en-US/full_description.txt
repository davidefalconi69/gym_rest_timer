A minimalist, distraction-free rest timer designed for the gym. No clutter, no ads, just a simple tool to track your rest intervals efficiently.

**Features**
- **Distraction-Free:** Single configurable timer with a clean, large display.
- **Live Notifications:** See your countdown directly in the notification tray.
- **Privacy First:** No internet permission, no analytics, no tracking. Your data stays on your device.
- **Battery Efficient:** Uses a lightweight background service to keep the timer running without draining your battery.
- **Theme Engine:** Choose from Light, Dark, AMOLED (True Black), or Monochrome themes.
- **Customizable:** Adjust sound, vibration, and default durations to match your workout style.
